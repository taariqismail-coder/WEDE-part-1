Website Project Proposal 1
Target Organisation: Lab 47 (Clothing Brand)
1. Organisation Overview

Name: Lab 47

Lab 47 is an independent streetwear clothing brand that focuses on modern youth fashion and creative culture founded by Taariq Ismail. The brand combines elements of streetwear, music culture, and urban design to produce clothing that reflects individuality and artistic expression.

Lab 47 was founded with the goal of creating unique clothing pieces that represent modern street culture. The brand focuses on producing high-quality apparel such as t-shirts, hoodies, and accessories that appeal to young people who are influenced by music, fashion, and digital culture.

The brand currently promotes its products mainly through social media platforms, where it shares designs, product drops, and creative visuals. However, the organisation aims to expand its online presence by developing a professional website where customers can learn more about the brand, view products, and contact the business for enquiries.

Mission Statement:

The mission of Lab 47 is to create stylish and creative streetwear clothing that represents individuality, youth culture, and artistic expression.

Vision Statement:

The vision of Lab 47 is to grow into a recognised streetwear brand that connects fashion, music, and creative culture while reaching customers both locally and internationally.

Target Audience:

The target audience includes:

Young adults aged 16–30

Individuals interested in streetwear fashion

Music and urban culture enthusiasts

Customers looking for unique and creative clothing

2. Website Goals and Objectives

The main goal of the Lab 47 website is to create a professional online platform that showcases the brand and its clothing products.

Objectives include:

Promoting Lab 47 clothing and brand identity

Allowing customers to view available products

Providing information about the brand and its story

Allowing customers to enquire about purchases or collaborations

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Success of the website will be measured through:

Visitor tracking will be implemented via Google Analytics once the website is live. This will allow us to monitor the number of visitors, page views, and other engagement metrics.

3. Current Website Analysis

Currently, Lab 47 does not have an official website and mainly relies on social media platforms to promote products and communicate with customers.

Strengths

Strong creative identity

Existing audience on social media

Weaknesses

No dedicated website for customers

Limited ability to organise product information

Difficult for new customers to discover the brand

Areas for Improvement

Creating a website will provide:

A professional online store presence

Better presentation of clothing products

Easier access for customers to contact the brand

4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality

The Lab 47 website will include the following pages:

Homepage (index.html)
A landing page introducing the brand with a hero image and navigation menu.

About Page (about.html)
Information about the brand’s story, mission, and vision.

Products Page (products.html)
A display of clothing items such as hoodies, t-shirts, and accessories.

Enquiry Page (enquiry.html)
A form where customers can enquire about purchasing products or collaborations.

Contact Page (contact.html)
Contact information and social media links.

5. Design and User Experience

Colour Scheme

The website will use the following colour palette:

Black

Red

White

These colours represent a bold streetwear aesthetic.

Typography

Headings will use bold modern fonts, while body text will use readable fonts such as Arial.

Layout and Design

The website will follow a modern and minimal layout with strong visual images of clothing products.

User Experience Considerations

Easy navigation between pages

Clear presentation of products

Fast loading pages

Low-fidelity wireframes will be developed to visualise the layout.

6. Technical Requirements

The website will be developed using HTML to demonstrate basic web development principles.

The project will require a domain name such as lab47.co.za and web hosting services.

7. Timeline and Milestones

Week 1 – Project proposal and planning
Week 2 – Content research and image preparation
Week 3 – Sitemap and website structure planning
Week 4 – HTML development of pages
Week 5 – Testing and improvements
