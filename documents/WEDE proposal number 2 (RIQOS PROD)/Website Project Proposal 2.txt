Website Project Proposal 2
Target Organisation: Riqo’s Production
1. Organisation Overview

Name: Riqo’s Production

Riqo’s Production is an independent music production brand founded by Taariq Ismail. The brand focuses on producing instrumentals and beats for artists within hip-hop, trap, and modern digital music genres.

The purpose of Riqo’s Production is to provide artists with high-quality beats and music production services that help them create professional and unique music projects. The production brand works with independent artists and content creators who require instrumentals for their music.

Currently, the brand connects with artists primarily through social media and direct communication. However, there is no dedicated website where artists can learn about the services offered or contact the producer professionally.

Developing a website will allow Riqo’s Production to present its services in a structured and professional manner, making it easier for artists to discover the brand and enquire about collaborations.

Mission Statement

The mission of Riqo’s Production is to produce high-quality beats and instrumentals that support artists in creating original music.

Vision Statement

The vision of Riqo’s Production is to grow into a recognised music production brand that collaborates with artists locally and internationally.

Target Audience

The target audience includes:

Independent rappers and vocalists

Music artists searching for beats

Content creators who need background music

Individuals interested in music production

2. Website Goals and Objectives

The main goal of the website is to create a professional platform where artists can learn about the services offered by Riqo’s Production.

Objectives include:

Promoting music production services

Presenting available beats and production work

Allowing artists to enquire about collaborations

Building a professional online identity for the brand

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Visitor tracking will be implemented via Google Analytics once the website is live. This will allow us to monitor the number of visitors, page views, and other engagement metrics.


3. Current Website Analysis

Currently, Riqo’s Production operates mainly through social media and personal communication with artists.

Strengths

Direct communication with artists

Existing portfolio of music production work

Weaknesses

No official website

Limited structured presentation of services

Areas for Improvement

Creating a website will provide a professional platform where artists can learn about services and contact the producer easily.

4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality

The website will include the following pages:

Homepage (index.html)
Introduction to the producer and overview of services.

About Page (about.html)
Information about the producer, founder, and production style.

Services Page (services.html)
Details about beat production and collaboration services.

Enquiry Page (enquiry.html)
A form where artists can enquire about purchasing beats or working together.

Contact Page (contact.html)
Contact details and social media links.

5. Design and User Experience

Colour Scheme

Black

Red

White

These colours represent creativity and modern music culture.

Typography

Bold modern fonts will be used for headings, while body text will use simple sans-serif fonts for readability.

Layout and Design

The website will have a clean and modern layout with clearly organised sections.

User Experience Considerations

Simple navigation menu

Clear service information

Fast loading pages

6. Technical Requirements

The website will be developed using HTML to demonstrate the fundamental structure of web pages.

The project will require a domain name and basic web hosting services.

7. Timeline and Milestones

Week 1 – Project planning and proposal development
Week 2 – Content research
Week 3 – Sitemap and website structure planning
Week 4 – HTML development
Week 5 – Testing and debugging

Web hosting: R1000 per year
Website development: R4500 once-off
Maintenance: R800 per year

Estimated total: R6450
