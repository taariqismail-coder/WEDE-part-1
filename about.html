About Us

Riqo’s Production is a luxury-driven music and creative production company founded by Taariq Ismail. Built on precision, vision, and artistic excellence, the brand operates at the intersection of sound, culture, and premium creative expression. We specialise in crafting high-end beats, refined soundscapes, and tailored audio experiences for emerging artists, creators, and forward-thinking brands. Every production is designed with intention — blending sonic identity, modern aesthetics, and industry-level quality. Riqo’s Production is more than a studio — it is a creative house where ideas are elevated into signature sound.

Mission

To deliver high-quality, innovative music and creative content that helps artists and brands shine.

Vision

To become a leading production studio that empowers creators and elevates urban music culture.
